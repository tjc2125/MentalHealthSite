Extra Resources for Me

Columbia Counseling and Psychological Services (CPS) offers a range of emotional and mental health support tools and resources. When experiencing an urgent mental health concern, call CPS at 212-854-2878, which is available 24/7, to reach a licensed mental health professional.

Columbia Health works with students, faculty, and staff to develop initiatives designed to support members of the campus community. To speak to someone about managing stress:
  1. Schedule an appointment with a mental health professional at Counseling and Psychological Services by calling 212-854-2878
  2. Schedule an appointment online with a health care provider at Medical Services or call 212-854-7426
  3. Contact Alice! Health Promotion at 212-854-5453 or alice@columbia.edu

Mindfulness and meditation are approaches to managing stress, anxiety, and depression.
  1. Columbia offers opportunities to practice meditation through CUIMC Student Health on Haven.
  2. Listen to a loving kindness meditation led by Dr. Aisha Holder of Columbia University Counseling and Psychological Services.
